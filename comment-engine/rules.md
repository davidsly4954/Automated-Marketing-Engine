# Platform Rules & Ban Avoidance

## Universal Rules (All Platforms)

1. **Never automate posting.** Draft with AI, post manually. Always.
2. **Never mention your product in comments** unless someone specifically asks what you use or what you built. Even then, disclose you're the founder.
3. **90/10 rule** — 90% genuine helpful participation, 10% self-promotion at absolute most.
4. **No identical content across platforms.** Each comment should be unique to the thread context.
5. **Edit every AI draft** before posting. Add personal experience, adjust tone, introduce natural imperfections. AI-polished writing gets 127 views on Reddit; casual writing gets 1,400 (documented Medium experiment).

## Reddit

### Account Rules
- **Karma before promotion:** Need 50+ karma before any self-promotion. Many subreddits require 250-500+ karma to post.
- **Account age matters:** New accounts posting links in the first 2 weeks face 90%+ ban rate.
- **DO NOT mention your product for at least the first 10 days.** Build genuine karma first.
- **Never link directly to your domain** from your account until you have established credibility. If someone asks, mention the product name and let them Google it.

### Subreddit Research
Before posting in any subreddit, check:
- Sidebar rules for self-promotion policies
- Top posts to understand expected tone and format
- Whether they have dedicated promo threads (e.g., weekly feedback threads)
- Subscriber count and moderation style

### Detection & Ban Risks
- Reddit's ML models identify coordinated promotional campaigns across accounts even with varied patterns and different IPs.
- Reddit actively detects AI-generated text. Deliberately casual writing outperforms polished writing.
- Enforcement tiers: shadow ban → subreddit ban → account suspension → **domain-level spam flag** (your domain auto-removed sitewide) → IP restrictions.
- **Domain-level flag is nearly irreversible** and would permanently close Reddit as a channel.

### Safe Commenting Patterns
- 3-5 comments/day across 2-3 subreddits
- Space comments 15-30 minutes apart (don't batch-post)
- Vary comment length (some short, some detailed)
- Reply to replies on your comments (shows genuine engagement)
- Upvote other good answers in threads you comment on

## LinkedIn

### Account Rules
- **Daily limits (safe envelope):**
  - 15-20 connection requests/day (stay under 100/week)
  - 50-80 messages/day
  - 20-30 comments/day
  - 1-2 posts/day (algorithm promotes only 1 post per 24 hours)
- **Start at 50% volume and ramp over 2-3 weeks** on a new account.
- **Enable Creator Mode** for extended reach.

### What Works
- Comments that spark replies from the OP get **15x more algorithmic distribution** than likes.
- Hook first sentence with an insight or stance, not a compliment.
- Dwell time (time spent reading) is the #1 algorithm signal. Dense, substantive comments get distributed.
- Engaging with target prospects' posts for 2 weeks → connection request in week 3 → value-first message in week 4 converts at **25-50%**.

### What Gets You Banned
- Automated following/unfollowing
- Sending identical messages at scale
- Using tools that bypass OAuth (browser automation with cookies)
- Connection request spam (over 100/week triggers review)

## Hacker News

### Account Rules
- **Build karma for 30-60 days** before attempting a Show HN.
- **501 karma** = ability to downvote (rough proxy for "established member").
- Personal username, not corporate. HN is for people, not brands.
- **Transparent pricing is non-negotiable.** Hiding it is treated as a red flag.

### What Works
- Submit interesting articles relevant to your niche (builds karma).
- Comment with technical depth — cite specifics, real metrics, first-hand experience.
- Reply within 30 minutes of posts going live — early comments get exponentially more visibility.
- Best posting times: 8-10 AM ET, Tuesday-Thursday.

### What Gets You Banned
- Voting rings (if 5 people click a shared link and upvote immediately, votes are discarded and accounts flagged).
- Domain bans are permanent.
- Any perceived astroturfing or coordinated promotion.
- Shallow comments that don't add substance.

### Show HN Rules (for when you're ready)
- You must have personally built the product.
- Users must be able to try it immediately.
- Stay present in the thread answering questions for 4-6 hours.
- Zero superlatives, zero marketing language. Technical and factual only.
- Frame: "Show HN: [YourCompany] — [specific one-line description]"

## Twitter/X

### Account Rules
- **X Premium ($8/mo) is non-negotiable.** Premium accounts get ~10x algorithmic reach. Premium replies get priority placement.
- Free API tier: 500 posts/month (~17/day) — more than enough.
- Posts with external links get **94% reach suppression.** Put links in a reply, not the main post.
- Use 1-2 hashtags maximum per post (more than 2 causes 17% engagement drop).

### What Works
- Expert replies to larger accounts in your niche (visible to their entire audience).
- Threads (5-10 tweets) deliver 3x more engagement.
- React to breaking news in your space within 30 minutes.
- Spend 80% of time engaging with others' content, 20% posting your own (first 2-4 weeks).

### What Gets You Banned
- Automated following/unfollowing (single most enforced rule).
- Duplicate content across accounts.
- Automated DMs of any kind.
- Browser automation bypassing OAuth.
- X suspends **entire account networks** when one is caught.
